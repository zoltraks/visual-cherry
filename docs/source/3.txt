For .NET use ignore white without multiline or singleline options.
For other uses global (g) and extended (x) for pattern whitespace flags will be needed.

For .NET you don't need to escape slash or double quotes.
It is however needed for PCRE (PHP), ECMAScript (JavaScript) or Python.
